ABOUT Me

Frontend Developer & UI/UX Designer

I'm a passionate Frontend Developer and UI/UX Designer with over 3 years of experience building responsive websites and user-friendly interfaces. I specialize in HTML, CSS, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript, React, Adobe XD, and Figma — blending clean code with modern design tools to deliver smooth and engaging user experiences.

My approach combines performance-focused development with creative design, ensuring that every project is both technically sound and visually compelling. I thrive in collaborative environments and constantly seek to learn and adopt the latest trends in frontend technologies.
